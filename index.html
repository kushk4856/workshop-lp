Everything you need to know about EVOLVE'25

Evolve'25 is India's biggest AI-focused business and marketing event, hosted by AiSensy in collaboration with Meta. It brings together 1500+ leaders, innovators, and decision-makers to explore how AI is reshaping business growth.

Evolve'25 is scheduled for October 28, 2025, at the Hilton Garden Hotel, Manyata Business Park, Bengaluru. The event will run from morning through evening with multiple tracks, workshops, and networking sessions.

Evolve'25 is an in-person event. However, all ticket holders will receive recordings of the sessions after the event, allowing you to revisit key insights and share them with your team.

The event covers practical AI implementation including Marketing AI, Voice/Agentic AI, Hands-on Coding Workshops, Prompt Engineering, AI-powered automation, WhatsApp Business strategies, and real-world case studies from successful AI implementations.

Evolve'25 is perfect for founders, CEOs, marketing leaders, growth professionals, product managers, developers, and anyone interested in implementing AI solutions to scale their business. Whether you're an AI beginner or expert, you'll find value in the workshops and networking opportunities.

  • Networking with top AI founders and innovators
  • Access to the AI Startup Showcase
  • Lunch, high tea, and refreshments
